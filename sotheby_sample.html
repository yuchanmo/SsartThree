Sotheby’s is honored to host The Contemporary Austin’s 2021 Art Auction benefitting the museum’s thought-provoking exhibitions and award-winning programs.

 

Please be aware of the Conditions of Sale when bidding. As a benefit auction, there is no buyer’s premium charged. The only additional costs due to the winning bidder are applicable sales tax and shipping. Works auctioned are sold “as is,” and condition reports are included with lot descriptions as available.

 

In-person previews of the auction artwork will be available at The Contemporary Austin at 3809 W 35th St. Austin, TX 78703 on Friday, October 15 from 9am-5pm. Please contact Alyssa Miller to schedule. 

 

Please note that while this auction is hosted on Sothebys.com, it is being administered by The Contemporary Austin (“the museum”), and all post-sale matters (inclusive of invoicing and property pickup/shipment) will be handled by the museum. As such, Sotheby’s will share the contact details for the winning bidders with the museum so that they may be in touch directly post-sale.
